Who I Am

Hi! I'm a junior at the University of Iowa, double majoring in Business Analytics and Finance. I recently accepted a Business Analytics internship at Sammons Financial Group in Des Moines for this upcoming summer, and I’m excited to gain real-world experience in the field.

I grew up in Johnston, Iowa, and previously worked at Hyperion Field Club. College has been an awesome experience — from making new connections to discovering more about who I want to be, both personally and professionally.

Fun fact: when I was 12 years old, I played in the Little League World Series. It’s an experience I’ll never forget and one that taught me a lot about teamwork and perseverance.
